application in full edition

You should download the application from the first floor, fill it out and send it to us.

In case you cannot open the link, here is the full edition of the application.
But as long as you can download, please use the WORD edition.
Thanks.

Application for
Project for Community Involvement
2005


This application and process is only used for year 2005-2006, preliminary board.
New applications will be used and election process outlined in Constitution will be followed for the following years.

The application is reviewed at a rolling basis. But please apply soon, or the position you are applying for may be already filled. We take more than one person for each position.
Filled application should be sent to rocu_pci@hotmail.com. Applications sent to any other places will not be considered.
Remember that an “interview” will be required, mainly through internet. A current ROCU officer will contact you and set up a time for online chatting. (as long as you have QQ, msn, skype, etc.) The interview is just for reference so that we know who you are, instead of guessing who is behind the computer screen. It’ll be very relaxing.

---------------------------------------------Personal Information------------------------------------Name (in both Chinese and English): ___________________
Gender: __________________
Date of Birth: ___________________
Email Address: ________________________________
Mailing Address: ______________________________________________________________
Permanent Address (if not the same as mailing address):
_______________________________________________________________________________
Permanent Address Phone Number: _______________________
Preferred Phone Number (office or cell phone): _______________________________


--------------------------------------------Education Information------------------------------------
List schools you went to since 6th grade. (starting from the most recent)
Year (eg.2005-2006)              School Name (including city, province, country)
_________________     ___________________________________________
_________________      ___________________________________________
_________________      ___________________________________________


-------------------------------------------------Position Preference----------------------------------
Please rank your preferences of the positions you are applying for. (You can apply for as many positions as you want.)
Before applying, please refer to Article Ⅳ to be acquainted with the committee structure and responsibilities of different positions.

__ Administrative Chair
__ Treasurer
__ Public Events Chair
__ Development Chair
__ Publicity Chair

How much time can you devote to PCI every week?
____
(Don’t worry. The answer doesn’t matter. It simply helps us to allocate tasks and assign groups.)

-------------------------------------------------------Essay Questions-------------------------------
Feel free to attach any resume with the application. It’ll be very helpful. The resume you use to apply for colleges will be perfectly fine, and we may give you some suggestions on how to refine it so that your colleges will like it &#61514;


1.       Explain your preferences. Why do you want to do that job most?
2.       List any of your experience that qualifies you for that job.
3.       How do you visualize the future of ROCU? What should be done and what should be avoided?
4.       Are you clear with what your job is and do you have any plan of what to do?

One thing we do not ask and we assume you have is PPP--Passion, Patience and Perseverance.

We know that you are busy and really don’t want to write any essay, so this is how we make it much easier. Think about the questions briefly and no need to write it down, let’s talk about your answers during the interview.
But still, if you would like to send written answers, it’ll be more than welcomed. Remember, all the answers may not exceed two pages.


Applicant: ______________________________
Date: ________________________________
By signing my name above, I agree with the Constitution of ROCU, and am subject to all its disciplines.
