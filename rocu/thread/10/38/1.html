A judgment made before all the facts are known must be called ------- .


A.  harsh

B.  deliberate

C.  sensible

D.  premature

E.  fair


[glow=255,red,2]Answer
Press Ctrl+A[/glow]

Getting the correct answer to this question depends almost entirely on your knowing the definitions of the five words you must choose from. Which of the choices describes a judgment made “before all the facts are known?” Such a judgment, by definition, is not deliberate, and the sentence doesn&#39;t tell us whether the judgment was “harsh” or lenient, “sensible” or dumb, “fair” or unfair. “Premature” means hasty or early. It fits the blank perfectly.

A group of workers can harvest all the grapes from 10 square meters of a vineyard in 1/2 minute. At this rate, how many minutes will the group need to harvest all the grapes from 100 square meters of this vineyard?

A.  5

B.  10

C.  20

D.  50

E.  60


[glow=255,red,2]Answer
Press Ctrl+A[/glow]

Since 100 square meters is 10 times 10 square meters, 10 times as much time is needed to harvest the grapes from the 100 square meters of vineyard. 10 × (1/2) minute = 10/2, which equals 5 minutes.

JUNE 7, 2006
         
Read the following SAT test question, then click on a button to select your answer.

In a community of 416 people, each person owns a dog or a cat or both. If there are 316 dog owners and 280 cat owners, how many of the dog owners own no cat?

   (A)  36


   (B)  100


   (C)  136


   (D)  180


   (E)  316

[glow=255,red,2]Answer
Press Ctrl+A[/glow]
(C)is correct. To solve this problem, you must realize that some of the 316 dog owners also own cats and some of the 280 cat owners also own dogs. This has to be true because 316 + 280 is more than 416! The question asks how many of the dog owners do not own cats. To solve the problem, first find the number of people who own both a dog and a cat. To do this, let b equal the number who own both a dog and a cat. Then 416 = 316 + 280 – b. (Note: Adding 316 and 280 counts b twice, so b must be subtracted from the sum to get 416.) Next, the equation 416 = 596 – b can be solved for b, yielding b = 180. Finally, the number of dog owners who own no cat is 316 – 180 = 136.

If the graph of the function &#402; in the xy-plane contains the points (0, –9), (1, –4), and (3, 0), which of the following CANNOT be true?


A.  The graph of &#402; has a maximum value.

B.  y 0 for all points (x, y) on the graph of &#402;.

C.  The graph of &#402; is symmetric with respect to a line.

D.  The graph of &#402; is a line.

E.  The graph of &#402; is a parabola.


[glow=255,red,2]Answer
Press Ctrl+A[/glow]

If the graph of the function &#402;, which contains the points (0, –9), (1, –4), and (3, 0), were a line, then the slope of the segment connecting the points (0, –9) and (1, –4) would be the same as the slope of the segment connecting the points (1, –4) and (3, 0). However, the slope of the segment connecting the points (0, –9) and (1, –4) is 5, and the slope of the segment connecting the points (1, –4) and (3, 0) is 2. Therefore, the graph of &#402; cannot be a line.

The statements in the other four options could be true. For example, if the equation of &#402; were y = –(x – 3)^2, then the graph of &#402; would contain the points (0, –9), (1, –4), and (3, 0). The graph of &#402; would be a parabola symmetric with respect to the line with equation x = 3. The maximum value of &#402; would occur at the point (3, 0), and y  0 would be true for all points (x, y) on the graph of &#402;.
