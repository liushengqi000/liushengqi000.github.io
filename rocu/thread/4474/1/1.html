IVOR之本科签证20问 2006纪念版

  参考了05-06年共50余篇赴美本科签经后作的统计,这些问题的出现频率由1至20以次减弱。第一个问题共出现25次，而最后个问题只出现了6次。当然还有不少问题也有出现过，但并不普遍，所以此处没有记载。
  这是我签证前自制的准备材料，成功后又多次充实修改。鉴于网上传播的多为研究生及其他签证问题（如25问，108问等），对本科签证并不太实用。所以将此问置于网上，与各位签友共享，也请给位前辈指点。
  祝大家签证顺利。

1.  Why you want to study in the USA?
2.  Why did you choose this college?
3.  What do you want to do after graduation?
4.  What do you parents do?
5.  Have you been to the states before?
6.  What’s your major?
7.  Do you take GaoKao? What’s your score / May I see your admission ticket?
8.  Did you apply for any Chinese universities? What are they?
9.  What is the school you going to?
10. Do you have any documents on your family’s financial status?
11. Who will pay for you?
12. Why don’t you have your undergraduate study in China?
13. So you got scholarship?
14. Where is this College?
15. Show me your grade. Also your TOEFL and SAT?
16. What is your parent’s annual income?
17. May I see your offer letter/ FA letter?
18. How many colleges did you apply to US altogether? What are they? How many give you admission?
19. When you will graduate from High School?
20. How do you know this college?

以下是10个经典BT问题，仅供消遣：
1.  How old are you? Are you 15?
2.  Have you applied any university in Europe?
3.  You are going to lie to me, aren’t you?
4.  Why are you nervous?
5.  So you work for the TV station with a Bachelor’s degree?
6.  You speak English?
7.  Give me your GRE?
8.  What did you write in your essay?
9.  What’s the weather like out there?
10. Can you explain why 90% Chinese students didn’t come back?
