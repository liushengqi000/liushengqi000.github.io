10. PACKAGE AND PRESENT YOUR ESSAY APPROPRIATELY

  IF THE SCHOOL PERMITS IT, PRESENT YOUR ESSAY ON A SEPARATE SHEET OF PAPER

  NUMBER EACH PAGE OF YOUR ESSAY, AND INCLUDE YOUR NAME ON EACH PAGE

  ASSEMBLE YOUR ESSAYS PROPERLY

  BE SURE TO COMPLY STRICTLY WITH ANY PAGE LIMITS THAT THE SCHOOL IMPOSES

  DON&#39;T WORRY TOO MUCH ABOUT THE SCHOOL&#39;S WORD LIMITS

  DON&#39;T WRITE YOUR ESSAY IN LONGHAND

  USE HIGHLY READABLE FONTS

  INCLUDE AMPLE MARGINS

  PRINT YOUR FINAL ESSAY ON A HIGH-RESOLUTION LASER PRINTER

  DO NOT SUBMIT ESSAYS ON VIDEOTAPE OR AUDIOTAPE

  THINK TWICE BEFORE INCLUDING SUPPLEMENTARY MATERIALS
