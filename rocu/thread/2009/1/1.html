记录付费主题, 价格: 20
[/free]  I&#39;m in Kowloon, the international school. Since the school on the Pipa Hill, most students from mainland reach by taxi and with their luggage. They will check your printed admission ticket and ID card, but still take your Pass with you. Actually always with your pass is better, for some shop in Hongkong especially during National Holidays will give your some discount if showing your pass. Some informaiton which should be writtten on the answer sheet will be on the blackboard of the test room, or you can just ask the supervisor when the pass by you as I do. Feel free to ask any question during the test except the &quot;what does this word mean?&quot;

It took a long time to make everybody of the test room down and ready to start. They said they would spare no time for lated takers but they did. So don&#39;t be to desperate if you are lated. Make yourself calm and ready is the most important.

You can choose any seat you&#39;d like to, but remember there are three teachers and always moving like ghosts. Several pencils and sharp machines will be provided on the front desk. No else except pencils, erasers, calculator when on math and yourself may be shown over the desk.  Don&#39;t put your beverage on the desk since it&#39;s too dangerous and the desk is so tiny.

Three breaks in all(123,45,67,8910), range aroung 5 minutes. Also you do not need to be very hurry. They have enough patience to wait for everyone. The place was quite cool so take another clothing with you may be better.

Any time before starting a new section the main supervisor will make some time to read the direction and said&quot; don&#39;t start work until I tell you to do so.&quot; You can start, of course. This period of time if extremely important for reading section.

If you are caught working on the other section, the teacher will ban you honestly. Don&#39;t hope they will pretend to be have not seen. But you can do it again Actuallt it needs some technique to calculate the moving regular of the three people, and new taker as me are not recommended to do that. So for me, I worked three times on the math section

If you are lucky enough to have a reading section or writing as section 10. Congratulations! There are two special quesitons in the end to be answered and you may have some time to hesitate over the quesiton of section 10.

Last but not least, make full use of your break time thought it&#39;s so short. Always tell yourself this is a new starts and you can do better on the new section. And do not forget to take a glance at what&#39;s the next section for before break.

Last Last: good luck of 11SAT![free]
