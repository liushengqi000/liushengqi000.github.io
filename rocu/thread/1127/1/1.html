Yeshiva University 翻译成叶西瓦大学，原名叶西瓦学院。1886年建于纽约。叶西瓦大学是美国最古老的综合性大学之一. 叶西瓦大学是由犹太教信徒赞助所以也叫犹太大学. 该大学由18个本科和研究生学院组成分布四个在纽约的分校. 学科涉及到艺术, 科学和犹太文化和思想. 叶西瓦大学是毕业率最高的美国大学之一,其毕业率为92%排第九名.综合排名94名.

课程介绍:
本科有五个学院: 1, 叶西瓦学院. 2, Stern 女子学院, 3,商学院 4, 犹太教学院. 5, 亚不拉汗木以色列学院.
研究生学院: 1, 爱因斯坦医学院. 2, Cardozo 法学院. 3, Azrieli 犹太教育与管理学院. 4, Revel 犹太学习学院. 5, Ferkauf 心理学学院. 6, Wurzweiler 社会工作学院.


由于该大学接受犹太移民的赞助,所以其犹太教信徒学生将得到全部资助,针对国际学生,录取采取学术和宗教信仰双重考察录取. 如果符合学校申请条件的国际学生可以优先考虑,因为该大学资助相当相当多,并且可以比一般名校更顺利进入商界.

All foreign citizens applying to Stern College for Women, Yeshiva College, or Sy Syms School of Business should follow the same basic application requirements as US citizens. In order for any application to be processed, the Office of Admissions must receive the completed application, essay, high school transcript, and $50 admission fee in the form of a bank money order payable in US dollars. All students who are not native English speakers are required to take the TOEFL and achieve a score that reflects proficiency in English. International students are also required to submit Math SAT scores but may substitute the TOEFL for the verbal section of the SATs. All applicants must also have an interview with a YU Admissions officer, which applicants are required to schedule by calling the Admissions Office. Transcripts and copies of certificates qualifying the applicant for admission to university study must be officially translated into English (if not in English or Hebrew) and sent directly to the Office of Admissions.
Applications for the fall semester are processed in three stages. Applicants whose completed applications with all the supporting documentation and interview that are received by October 15th will be informed of a decision by December 15th, applicants who are complete by December 15th will be informed of a decision by February 15th, and applicants who are complete by February 15th will be informed of a decision by April 15th.
