A.  For both his shorter and longer

For the function &#402;, y = &#402;(x) is inversely proportional to x, where x &gt; 0. If &#402;(3) = 6, what is the value of &#402;(2)?


A.  4

B.  8

C.  9

D.  12

E.  18


[glow=255,red,2]Answer
Press Ctrl+A[/glow]

Since y = &#402;(x) is inversely proportional to x, it follows that &#402;(x) =c/x  for some constant c. You can use &#402;(3) = 6 to find the value of c:  c/3= 6, so c = 18. Therefore, &#402;(2)= 9.

If it takes 10 people 12 hours to do a certain job, how many hours would it take 6 people, working at the same rate, to do of the same job?


A.  6

B.  5

C.  4

D.  4

E.  3




[glow=255,red,2]Answer
Press Ctrl+A[/glow]

CORRECT! Here&#39;s why:

One approach is to find out each person’s individual working rate. Since 10 people do the job in 12 hours, each person can do 1/10 of the job in 12 hours, which means that each person, working alone, could do the whole job in 120 hours.

A group of 6 people would be able to do the job in 1/6 of the time, so the group would need 1/6 of 120 hours, or 20 hours, to do the job. If this group of 6 people only wanted to do 1/4 of the job, they would need 1/4 of that time, or 1/4 of 20 hours, which is 5 hours.

Difficulty: Hard
