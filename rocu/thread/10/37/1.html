The amount that a plumber charges for a service call that is h hours long is shown in the graph above. The charges for a service call consist of an initial amount plus a charge for each hour of work. According to the graph, what is the hourly charge?


A.  $25.00

B.  $37.00

C.  $40.00

D.  $50.00

E.  $75.00



[glow=255,red,2]Answer
Press Ctrl+A[/glow]

The plumber’s charge for 1 hour is the sum of the initial amount and the hourly charge, and the plumber’s charge for 2 hours is the sum of the initial amount and twice the hourly charge. Hence the hourly charge is equal to the charge for 2 hours, or $125, minus the charge for 1 hour, $75, which is equal to $125 – $75 = $50.

What is the volume of a cube with surface area 54x^2?


A.  9x^2

B.  27x^3

C.  81x^2

D.  81x^3

E.  729x^3



[glow=255,red,2]Answer
Press Ctrl+A[/glow]

Every cube has 6 identical square faces. Since the total surface area of this cube is 54x^2, each face has area 54x^2 /6 = 9x^2. Therefore, each edge has length(9x^2)^(1/2) , or 3x. The volume is (3x)3, or 27x^3.

A woman drove to work at an average speed of 40 miles per hour and returned along the same route at 30 miles per hour. If her total traveling time was 1 hour, what was the total number of miles in the round trip?


A.  30

B.  30

C.  34

D.  35

E.  40



[glow=255,red,2]Answer
Press Ctrl+A[/glow]

Let t represent the time it took the woman to drive to work. Since her total traveling time was 1 hour, the time it took her to return home is 1 – t. The distance in miles can be found using the formula distance = rate × time. The distance traveled to work is 40t, and the return distance is 30(1 – t). Since the distance to work is the same as the return distance, 40t = 30(1 – t). Solving for t yields t =3/7 . So the distance one way is 40 × (3/7) =120/7  = 17 (1/7)miles. The total number of miles in the round trip is 2(17(1/7)), or 34(2/7) miles.

If the diameter of a bicycle wheel is 0.5 meter, how many meters has the center of the wheel traveled when the wheel has made 3 complete revolutions along a straight road?

A.  

B.  3

C.  12

D.  

E.  25


[glow=255,red,2]Answer
Press Ctrl+A[/glow]

Since the diameter of the wheel is 0.5 meter or 1/2 meter, its circumference is  1/2 pie or 1pie/2. When the wheel has made 3 complete revolutions along a straight road, the center of the wheel will have traveled a distance equal to 3 times the circumference, that is, 3(pie/2) or 3pie /2.
