Read the following SAT test question, then click on a button to select your answer.

The sum, product, and average (arithmetic mean) of three integers are equal. If two of the integers are 0 and -5, the third integer is


A. -5

B. 0

C. 2

D. 5

E. 10



[glow=255,red,2]Answer[/glow]
用的白色字体，用鼠标选中看
You have three integers, 0, -5, and x, whose sum, product, and average are equal. Since 0 is one of the numbers, then the product is 0. So the sum and average are also equal to 0. Therefore -5 + x + 0 = 0, and x = 5.

The scatterplot above shows the enrollment in 1980 against the enrollment in 2000 for twenty colleges. Which of the labeled points represents the college that had the smallest percent increase in enrollment from 1980 to 2000?


A. A
B. B
C. C
D. D
E. E


[glow=255,red,2]Answer[/glow]

This is a scatterplot where each point represents a college. The x-coordinate is the enrollment in 1980, and the y-coordinate is the enrollment in 2000. Point E has approximate coordinates (7,500, 7,700), so the college represented by this point had an enrollment that increased only   0.027 = 2.7% between 1980 and 2000. All the other labeled points represent colleges with greater percent increases in enrollment.
