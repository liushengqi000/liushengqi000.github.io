哈佛：Once a student has completed one year of full-time study at a single college or university, he or she ordinarily may apply to Harvard only as a transfer student.

耶鲁：To be eligible to transfer, students must have at least one year and no more than two years of transferable credit by June of the current academic year. If by the end of the current academic year you will have completed less than one full year of full-time college study, you should apply as a freshman.
要不要隐瞒取决于你是不是读完了大一，还是第一结束前退学。

普林：there will be no transfer admission process until the fall of 2006, at the earliest.
普林科已不瞒，反正不收转学。

达特：International students attending non-U.S. colleges or universities must apply as International first-year candidates.
完全没有问题，可以不瞒。

但是我觉得我身边的人，多数都是瞒掉的。对于大部分学校，好像说如果学满一年了就必须申请transfer，当然申请的时候还没有满一年，但是学校是看你9月入学的时候有没有已经学完一年。我也奇怪：那么申请的时候说什么呢？说只要成功就马上退学？这个请soa出来解答一下。
