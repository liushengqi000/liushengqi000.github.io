&gt; greetings, the crowed was busy at handshaking. You stood there, eyes on

&gt; from God, I felt dizzy. We were not near, yet we were together.
&gt;

&gt; 近。

&gt; good-bye to accompany your flight. Gone was the plane, I suddenly tasted

&gt; shouldn ’t have hidden in the dark. I tried to forget your

&gt; 而不可。妾伸手欲触，无他，但枯叶一雨滴耳。
