个人情况介绍：美国私立高中，有奖学金，曾被拒签：（，家庭经济状况良好。

6.12,阴雨绵绵，Daddy Mummy 陪我在Victory Hotel住了一晚后，早晨7：50来到American Consulate门前等。
about 8:00,3号窗几个阿婆（事实上不到20岁）叫排队。So we queue in line.Then they gave numbers to us.前面几个叔叔阿姨都是1，2，3，but I got the number bigger than 150!!!!!!I was somewhat depressed,but still went in.
From 8:00 to 12:15,I wait and wait and wait and wait and wait......then about 12:15 一号窗 was opened by an American Uncle.He called the numbers in very bad Chinese,so I missed my number!!and he called again:&quot;xxx豪（号），青（请）干（赶）快赖（来）一号窗口排队！！”So I flew(somewhat 夸张）to the window.Below is our conversations:

M:Hello,sir!sorry to keep you waiting!!
V：(无表情）
M递材料，里面有我父母去过美国的照片，他似乎饶有兴趣地看）These are pictures of my parents taken in America.
Vh!beautiful(似乎和蔼可亲）
$%!@$.....?
M没听清，但硬着头皮回答)XXX state,XXX school.
V:No,I mean where r u studying NOW?
Mh!sorry.XXX school in Shenzhen.It&#39;s a very good school and most of the students in it are expected to enter the best universities in China,we......
V:STOP!!
M吐了吐舌头)Sorry.
V:Where is the university?
M:High School.
V:Sorry,high school.
M:XXX state.(见他没吭声),I don&#39;t think I am old enough to enter universities.(事实
上我已16)
V:It&#39;s difficult but.....possible.
M:yes.
V:So.....What&#39;s your favorite color?
M始料未及)Blue.
V:why?
M:Because I think this color is brighter.
V点点头，叽哩咕噜一大堆------听不见)
M:do you like blue ,too?(事实上我觉得这个问题是世界上最Stupid的问题之一)
V:No,I like !@#$@#!@(听不懂)
M.

随后他开始翻看我的材料，Admission letter,还有父母的银行存款证明等等，比较仔细。
突然，他拿着我前次被拒签的OF-156(B-2 visa)问：
V:Who&#39;s xxx(上次邀请我去美国的人）？
M:She&#39;s my friend.
V:How did you meet her?
M: I met her in my mother&#39;s friend&#39;s home(我相信我的答案跟上次是一样的)
V点点头)
M不敢出声)
V:why do you choose this school?
M:because it&#39;s a pretty good college-prepatory school,it&#39;s located in the surburb
area in XXX state and is quiet and beautiful.I think I can improve my study
there(full of grammar mistakes,但管不了那么多了)
V:why do you apply for high school in America?(他的意思大概是说为什么我不去读大学)
M:because I want to go to USA earlier so that I can prepare very well for my college study.I want to apply them(听起来很难听).
V:like?
M:Harvard University,Micheken University(别管考得上考不上，吹了牛再说)
V继续看我的材料，翻到我在学校的奖状时，叫住旁边一个像是华人的叫他翻译)
V:OK,take a seat and wait for 15 minutes.Then you can go out to get you visa.
M欣喜若狂)ok,thank you very much!!!(结果户口簿忘了拿，直到下午才跟护照一起被送出
来------大头虾！！)

听别人说高中生很难签，自己也想过可能暑假要再来。但没想到.......那个VO太好人了。慈眉善目的，对我很kind.以前还听说VO很凶呢！
这次成功可以说是出乎意料，因为我怕number 太大（都怪那几个阿婆，虽说她们帮了我),VO签到后来都烦了。但这个似乎是他一开窗口（一接班）第一个就是我，所以比较顺利。而且他看来心情挺好。
Daddy Mummy就没那么好彩了，在雨中等了3个多小时！！！！还好签到了，否则我真是无颜见他们。可怜天下父母心啊！！！
（签证前一天晚上捧着tigtag网友的签证经历看，增加了不少信心）
