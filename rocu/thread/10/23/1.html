At the Cross Street Convenience Store between 11 am and 1 pm on Friday, a total of n items were purchased. The graph above shows the distribution of the number of items purchased in each of five categories. In terms of n, how many more sandwiches than pastries were purchased?


A.  10n

B.  20n

C.  n/5

D.  n/10

E.   n/20



[glow=255,red,2]Answer
Press Ctrl+A[/glow]
Of the n items purchased, 30% were sandwiches and 10% were pastries. 30% of n is equal to 0.3n, and 10% of n is equal to 0.1n. Therefore, 0.3n – 0.1n = 0.2n, or n/5  more sandwiches than pastries were purchased.

Ignoring the growing accusations of ------- , Governor Anderson appointed yet another of his personal friends to a well-compensated government position.

Digital technology, like every marketer knows, it is synonymous with speed, precision, and the future.
