Section 3
Clause 1. Any Board member may resign by submitting a resignation letter to the Board. A resignation is deemed in effect after the Board’s approval.

Clause 2. In case of misconduct or negligence, a Board member may be impeached. The Process involves the following:
A.       a motion signed by
ⅰat least four members of the Board and twenty active members of ROCU, or
ⅱby at least forty voting member of ROCU.
B.       A special voting session on the matters attended by at least fifty voting members of ROCU.
C.       The board member in question shall be informed of the time and duration of this voting session a week in advance.
D.       Both the initiator of the motion and the Board member in question shall have the floor before voting begins. Failure of the board member in question to represent himself before the voting session ends shall take away his/her right to defend for himself.
E.       A two-thirds majority in the voting session is needed to complete the impeachment process.

Clause 3 In case of resignation, impeachment, or any other voluntary or involuntary vacancy in the Board, the Board shall designate the duties of the vacant position to the person with the next most votes in the last election.

Article Ⅵ. Meetings
Section 1. Board meeting shall be held on a regular basis, at least once a month, for the purpose of determining policy, activities, programs, and other business. The Chief Secretary is responsible for board meetings.
Committee meetings shall be held on a regular basis, at least once a month, for the purpose of overseeing ongoing activities and making future plans. All committee chairs are responsible for their own committee meetings.

Section 2. The Chief Secretary, Presidents or any officer chairing a meeting, is responsible for preparing an agenda in coordination with other members before the meeting.

Section 3. The Chief Secretary and Presidents hold the right to call for a meeting provided that two of them consider it necessary.

Section 4. Open meetings between the Board and members of ROCU shall be held from time to time at local areas, mostly in summer vacations. Notice of regular and special meetings shall be communicated to all members of the group by the Administrative Committee.

Section 5. All meetings shall be recorded by committee members in turn.
