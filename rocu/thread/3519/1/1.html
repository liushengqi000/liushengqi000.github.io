Princeton Professor Says No to Harvard Presidency

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Popular Princeton Professor will remain on campus.

(excerpt)

&quot;Renowned prospect won&#39;t join Harvard
Keohane pleased at Princeton post
By Marcella Bombardieri, Boston Globe Staff

To some professors on campus and Harvard-watchers across the country, Nannerl O. Keohane would be the perfect president for Harvard University.

As a former president at both Duke University and Wellesley College, she is one of the most highly regarded leaders in American higher education. As a member of Harvard&#39;s governing board, she knows as well as anyone the unique and momentous task of repairing the university after the troubling fall of Lawrence H. Summers.

But tear up those candidate lists, and hold back the nomination letters.

In a telephone interview yesterday, Keohane said she does not want the job.
- - -
&#39;&#39;I&#39;m not available,&quot; she said. &#39;&#39;I want to tell people to please stop putting me on the lists of potential candidates.&quot; - - -

She stepped down from Duke in 2004, and has returned to teaching and research as a professor at Princeton University this school year.&quot;
