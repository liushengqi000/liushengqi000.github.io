What is the area of the triangle in the figure above?


A.  4.0

B.  7.5

C.  8.0

D.  8.5

E.  15.0


[glow=255,red,2]Answer
Press Ctrl+A[/glow]

CORRECT! Here&#39;s why:

Figures in a Coordinate Plane
The figure provides all the information you need to answer the question.

What Is the Question Asking?
You are asked to figure out the area of a triangle that is defined by the points in a coordinate plane.

What Do You Know?
The triangle in the figure is a right triangle with the right angle at the lower left.

Because it is a right triangle, its base and height are the two sides that form the right angle.

The area of a triangle is 1/2bh.

The base of the triangle extends from point (0, 0) to point (5, 0). So it is 5 units long.

The height of the triangle extends from point (0, 0) to point (0, 3). So it is 3 units long.

Area = 1/2bh

=1/2 (3)(5)

=1/2 (15)

= 7.5

In the figure, the slope of the line through points P and Q is 3/2. What is the value of k?


A.  4

B.  5

C.  6

D.  7

E.  8



[glow=255,red,2]Answer
Press Ctrl+A[/glow]

The slope of a line in a coordinate plane is given by the fraction whose numerator is the change in y between any two points on the line and whose denominator is the change in x between the same points on the line.

The question asks for the value of k, which is the x-coordinate of point Q.

The change in y between points P and Q is 7 – 1 = 6. The change in x between these points is k – 1. Since the slope is 3/2, it follows that 6/(k-1) =3/2 . Solving this equation gives 3k – 3 = 12. Therefore, 3k = 15, and k = 5.

Difficulty: Medium

Each of 5 men played a game of chess with each of 5 women and then each woman played a game of chess with each of the other women. How many games of chess were played?


A.  20

B.  25

C.  35

D.  45

E.  50


[glow=255,red,2]
Answer
Press Ctrl+A[/glow]

Each man will play 1 game of chess with each of the 5 women. Therefore, each man will play 5 games. Since there are 5 men, a total of 25 games will be played between the men and the women. Each woman will play 1 game of chess with each of 4 other women. In counting the number of games played, it is important to avoid counting any game more than once. The first woman will play 4 games. The second woman has already played the first woman, so she will play 3 additional games with the other women. Similarly, there are 2 more games played by the third woman, and 1 more game played by the fourth woman. All the games played by the fifth woman have already been counted. So the 5 women play a total of 4 + 3 + 2 + 1, or 10 games with each other. Thus, a total of 35 games of chess have been played.

In the xy-plane, line l is perpendicular to the graph of the function &#402;(x) = 5x – 2. Line  could be the graph of which of the following functions?


g(x) = –5x
g(x) = -1/5x
g(x) = x – 2
g(x) = 1/5x
g(x) = 5x



[glow=255,red,2]Answer
Press Ctrl+A[/glow]
If two lines are perpendicular, the product of their slopes is equal to –1. The function &#402;(x) = 5x – 2 is in slope-intercept form, so the slope of the graph of &#402;(x) = 5x – 2 is equal to 5. Therefore, the slope of line l must be equal to -1/5. The only choice that corresponds to a slope of  is -1/5. g(x) = -1/5x.

Difficulty: Hard
